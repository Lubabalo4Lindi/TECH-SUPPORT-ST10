Common Troubleshooting Questions and Answers

Q: Why is my computer running slow?

A: Slow performance can result from too many programs running, insufficient memory, or a malware infection. Try closing unnecessary applications, running a virus scan, and clearing temporary files.

Q: Why can’t I connect to the internet?

A: Check if your Wi-Fi is enabled, restart your router, and confirm that cables are properly connected. If problems persist, contact your internet service provider.

Q: Why does my screen keep freezing?

A: Screen freezes can occur due to overheating, outdated drivers, or hardware issues. Restart the computer and ensure it’s in a well-ventilated area. Update your software and drivers regularly.

Q: Why won’t my printer connect to my computer?

A: Make sure the printer is powered on and properly connected to the computer or network. Check for driver updates and ensure that your computer recognizes the device.

Q: What should I do if my software crashes frequently?

A: Frequent crashes may be due to compatibility issues or corrupted files. Reinstall the software, check for updates, and ensure your operating system is up-to-date.
