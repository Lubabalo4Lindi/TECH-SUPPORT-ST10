Steps for Issue Escalation

Our structured escalation procedure ensures timely and effective responses to critical issues. If a problem cannot be resolved at the initial support level, it will be escalated through the following levels to ensure a resolution:

1. Tier 1 Support (Initial Contact)

Basic troubleshooting is performed by the Tier 1 support team. If the issue is beyond their capabilities, they will escalate it to Tier 2.

2. Tier 2 Support (Technical Experts)

Tier 2 involves more experienced support staff who handle complex issues. They will perform advanced diagnostics and, if necessary, escalate the case further.

3. Tier 3 Support (Subject Matter Experts)

This tier consists of specialists or product experts who handle intricate issues requiring in-depth expertise. If a resolution still isn't found, the issue will be escalated to management.

4. Escalation to Management

If all technical resources have been exhausted, the case will be escalated to the support management team for critical review. Management may allocate additional resources or escalate to executive oversight.

Escalation Timelines

  • Low Priority: Response within 48 hours
  • Medium Priority: Response within 24 hours
  • High Priority: Immediate response and escalation

Communication and Follow-Up

Throughout the escalation process, customers will receive updates on the status of their issue and expected resolution times. Our team remains committed to transparency and regular communication.
